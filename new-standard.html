About this kit

 

 

Rebuilt for more rallies!

Beefier frame, Grippier balls, more intuitive installation

 

Easy to learn

After 10 minutes you’ll have some amazing rallies and MONSTER spikes

 

Play anywhere

All you need is 4 people and some open space. Play on grass, sand, in the basement, you name it.

 

Easy to transport

When disassembled the entire game fits into a lightweight carrying bag. Easy to take to the beach, campground, friend's house, any place good for playing.

 

Technically, yes, it is exercise.

Your significant other/mom/life coach can breathe a sigh of relief. Roundnet is a great form of exercise and is used by many athletes for cross-training.

 

 

What's in the box?

All you need to play is included:

- New & Improved Standard Spikeball set (incl. net)

- 2 balls

- Rule book

- Carrying bag for easy transport
